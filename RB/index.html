My Promise to You

This Raksha Bandhan, I don’t just accept your rakhi —
I accept the love, the trust, and the bond it represents.

A promise to stand by you —
not just during the happy times,
but when things get quiet, tough, or uncertain.

No matter where life takes us,
no matter how much changes —
one thing will always remain the same:
you’ll never face anything alone.

I’m your brother —
not just today, but for a lifetime.

Sure, I could buy you a gift —
something nice, maybe even expensive.
But Raksha Bandhan isn’t about money.
It’s about meaning.

This message comes from the heart.
I care about you deeply, and I always will.
No matter how hard life gets,
no matter what situation we’re in,
I’ll be there for you.

Because being your brother is more than just a bond —
it’s a blessing I carry with pride.

Always. Forever.
